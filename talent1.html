"Anyone" Videogame

On my second year taking programming, we had to make a project, so my team and I decided to make a game similar to "Don't Starve". The plot of the game was: You wake up in a hospital without remembering anything, not even your name. You see that you are in a small city, kind of a town but you find no one. As you investigate you see that a virus spread rappidly through the world, making everyone forgetting about their entire lives. After 7 days, the people started to dissapear, leaving no trace or clue behind. You get the conlusion that if you don't remember your life, you will also disapear. Your goal is to solve puzzles to unlock possible items connected to your life and remember who you are, before you disapear.
