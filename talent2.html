About it

I started to get into planes when I was about 15 years old. I would play videogames in my console, cell phone, and computer. One day I decided to buy a flight pack that had a flight stick, throttle and peddals. Got the cheapest one and I started to learn how to fly an airplane.

Experiences

Experience in combat

I got a flying pack to play War Thunder, a vehicle combat game. I would play mainly aircraft and would fight with a flying pack.

Experience in taking off and landing

With the same flight pack and game I would learn how to land and take off in an aircraft

Experience in adaptation

In War Thunder, every airplane is different, it as his own pros and cons. I had to learn how to use different aircraft and how to fight other aircraft.
